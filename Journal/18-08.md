  (define (iter result)
    (if (good-enough? result (improve result))
      result
      (iter (improve result))))
  (lambda (guess) (iter guess)))`

- My 'every' function:
```scheme
(define (every fun snt)
  (if (eq? (rest snt) '())
    (list (fun (first snt)))
    (append
      (list (fun (first snt)))
      (every fun (rest snt)))))
```
